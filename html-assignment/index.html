About Me

My name is Aldana. I am from Argentina. I lived in the capital city, but I moved with my boyfriend to Ottawa last July. I have been studying Multimedia Art for three years, yet I did not finish it because I preferred to study English. Because the MUTEK festival is presented in Argentina, I knew it was taking place in Montreal, Canada. Thus, I decided to study interactive media design in Canada.

In addition, I had the possibility to work for GMC and Ford as a content editor. I have learnt about XML coding, Photoshop, and business platforms. These international publicity agencies gave me the opportunity to grow professionally. However, I wanted to finish Multimedia Art in order to have more opportunities to work in other fields.
